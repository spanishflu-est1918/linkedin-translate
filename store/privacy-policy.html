Privacy Policy

DeLinked · Last updated: March 2026

Overview

DeLinked is a Chrome extension that translates LinkedIn posts into plain language using an AI API. This policy explains what data the extension handles.

What we collect

Nothing. DeLinked does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data about you. No account required. No analytics. No tracking.

Post text processing

When you hover over a LinkedIn post, the visible text of that post is sent to our translation API (linkedin-translate.spanishflu.workers.dev) to generate a translation. The text is used solely to produce the translation and is not stored or logged.

Local storage

The extension caches translations in your browser's sessionStorage so posts you've already hovered don't re-fetch. This data lives only in your browser and is cleared when you close the tab. Your optional API URL setting is stored via chrome.storage.sync (synced across your Chrome profile only).

Third-party services

The translation API is hosted on Cloudflare Workers. Post text is processed in-flight and not retained. See Cloudflare's privacy policy for their data handling practices.

Host permissions

The extension requests access to linkedin.com only. It does not access any other websites or domains.
